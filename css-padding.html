Different Paddings

Friendship is a divine relationship.We don't have similarity in our blood.
but still,that person cares for us.Irrespective of all differences,a friend
chooses you.understands you,and support you.
whenever you are in self-doubt or lacking
confidence,talk to a friend,and your worry
will surely go away.
