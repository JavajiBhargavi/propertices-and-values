The City

Delhi,officially the National Capital Territory(NCT)of Delhi,is a city and a union territory of india containing new delhi,the capital of india.Stradding the yamuna river,but spread chiefly to the west,or beyond its right bank.

Delhi shares borders with the state of Uttar Pradesh in the east and with the state of haryana in the remaining direction.
